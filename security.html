If your house is on fire what are the most likely things you will grab before leaving? It is highly likely that one of those things is actually something containing digital storage such as a computer, laptop, tablet or hard drive. So if these digital files are so important then they need to be protected from all forms of danger including IT Threats such as hackers and cyber attackers.
